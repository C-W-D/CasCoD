(B) 01/09/2023
(C) 01/09/1948
(D) 01/08/1987
(E) 03/17/1987
(F) 01/09/1987

Right Response: If today is 02/01/1987, then one week from today is 02/08/1987. However, since we are formatting the date in the UK style, the date would be 01/09/1987. Therefore, the answer is (F).

Wrong Response: If today is 02/01/1987, then one week from today is 02/08/1987. However, since we are formatting the date in the UK style, the answer should be 08/02/1987. Therefore, the answer is (D).
